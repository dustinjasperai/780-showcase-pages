Quick walkthrough
The scripts

3 Video Scripts + 2 Static Ads

All five drive registrations to your existing "Launch & Grow a Successful Cash Clinic" masterclass. You film the video scripts on your phone in your clinic. We handle the rest.

Script 1: The Burnout Hook

"If you're a physical therapist seeing 18 patients a day in 15-minute slots and wondering why you're burned out, it's not you. It's the business model."

On-camera, 55-70 seconds. Walks through the insurance trap, introduces the cash-based alternative, and drives to masterclass registration. Broadest audience. Best for cold traffic.

Script 2: The Money Math Hook

"A good haircut costs more than what most insurance companies reimburse for a physical therapy visit. Let that sink in for a second."

On-camera, 45-55 seconds. Breaks down the per-visit economics of insurance vs. cash. Concrete numbers. Appeals to analytical PTs who need math before emotion.

Script 3: The Patient Care Hook

"I became a physical therapist to help people. Then I spent three years watching insurance companies decide what my patients actually got."

On-camera, 50-60 seconds. Mission-driven angle. Connects cash-based practice to better patient outcomes. Targets PTs who care more about clinical quality than income.

Script 4: Static Image Ad (Evergreen)

"See 8 Patients a Day. Earn More Than You Do Now."

Static feed ad. Year-round. Professional image of Aaron in clinic with text overlay. Drives masterclass registrations from feed scroll.

Script 5: Static Image Ad (Burnout Angle)

"Before You Leave PT, Watch This Free Masterclass."

Static ad targeting PTs showing burnout signals. Split image concept (chaos vs. calm clinic). Retargeting layer for video viewers who didn't register.

Full script body copy for all five ads available on request. You film the three video scripts on your phone in your clinic or office. No production crew needed. We handle editing, ad build, targeting, and optimization.
