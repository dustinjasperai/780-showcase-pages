What Is Missing Right Now

Zero Meta Ads

No current or historical Facebook or Instagram campaigns. Franchise competitors with ad budgets are capturing the homeowners who search online, see an ad, and book the first professional company they find.

Two Instagram Accounts

@services_homa_ (369 followers) and @homa_services_ (231 followers). Combined: roughly 600 followers. Two accounts split the audience, confuse customers, and weaken brand presence. Should be one consolidated account.

No Video Content Strategy

Christmas lights are one of the most visual home services. Before/after videos, time-lapse installs, and drone shots of lit-up homes sell the service without saying much. This content does not exist yet.

No French-Language Ads

The entire Gatineau market is untapped by paid ads. No competitor is running French-language Meta ads for Christmas light or permanent lighting installation in this area. That is rare and valuable.

No CRM or Follow-Up System

Leads need follow-up within minutes, not days. Without automated text and email follow-up, leads go cold and get booked by faster competitors.

Celebright Under-Marketed
