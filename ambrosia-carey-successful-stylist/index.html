Script 2: The Pricing Confidence Hook (35-50 sec)

"Most stylists price their services based on what the person next to them charges. That's why most stylists never break six figures."

Here's what happens. You start at a salon, you look at the price menu, and you think "that's about right." You raise your prices when the salon does. Maybe you add $5-10 after a few years. But you never actually calculate what your services need to cost for you to hit your income goals. When I started tracking my numbers, I realized I was leaving thousands of dollars on the table every month. Not because I needed more clients. Because I was undercharging the ones I already had. I built a system around this that I teach in the Successful Stylist Academy. It's not about raising prices and hoping clients don't leave. It's about understanding your value, communicating it clearly, and building a brand that attracts the clients who are happy to pay what you're worth. I went from financial hardship to Top 5 Colorist nationally and a 7-figure salon using this framework.
