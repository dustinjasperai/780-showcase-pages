Our Story

26 Years on 14th Street. Same Owner, Same Standards.

Kevin Scarrott opened Southwest Auto Service in 1999 with a straightforward idea: be honest about what a car needs, charge a fair price, and do the work right the first time.

Twenty-six years later, that approach has earned over 500 five-star reviews and a loyal base of customers who keep coming back, some for over a decade. Kevin and his team, including Jared and a crew of experienced technicians, treat every vehicle like it belongs to a neighbor, because in this part of Calgary, it usually does.

Southwest Auto is also connected to Glen Meadows Auto Service on 37th Street SW, giving Kevin's team the depth and resources to handle anything from a simple oil change to a complex engine rebuild.
