About Kevin's Shop

Same Owner. Same Standards. 26 Years Running.

Kevin Scarrott opened Southwest Auto Service in 1999 on 14th Street SW with a straightforward approach: tell people what their car actually needs, give them a price upfront, and do the work right.

Twenty-six years later, that hasn't changed. Kevin and his team, including Jared, have built a base of repeat customers who come back year after year. Some, like Jessica, have trusted the shop for over a decade. Others found Kevin through a CAA recommendation and never went anywhere else.

Southwest Auto also operates alongside Glen Meadows Auto Service on 37th Street SW, giving the team the depth and resources to handle everything from a basic oil change to a complex engine rebuild.
