5 Ad Scripts for Your Book Funnel

Here are the ready-to-film ad scripts I mentioned in my email. They push PT practice owners into your existing free book download. The book does the selling. Your email nurture does the warming. These just fill the top.

Quick walkthrough
The scripts

3 Video Scripts + 2 Static Ads

All five drive downloads of The Practice Freedom Method. You film the video scripts on your phone. We handle the rest.

Script 1: The 137 Days Off Hook

"In 2013 I took 137 days off from my physical therapy practice. I didn't treat a single patient. And I made more money that year than any year before it."

On-camera, 55-70 seconds. Your strongest unique claim. Nobody else in the space can say this. Tells the fire story, introduces the system, drives to free book download.

Script 2: The "Working 60 Hours" Hook

"If you're working 60 hours a week in your own practice and making less than you'd make as an employee somewhere else, something is broken. And it's not your work ethic."

On-camera, 45-55 seconds. Pain point targeting. Broadest audience. Every overwhelmed owner-operator relates to this immediately.

Script 3: The Exit Premium Hook

"I sold my physical therapy practice for 66% over market value. The buyer didn't pay that premium because of my patients or my location. They paid it because the business ran without me."

On-camera, 50-60 seconds. Exit-focused. Targets owners 40-60 thinking about what their practice is worth. Highest intent, highest ticket audience.

Script 4: Static Ad (137 Days)

"137 Days Off. More Revenue Than Ever. Here's the Book."

Static feed ad. The "137 days" number is the scroll-stopper. Drives free book downloads. Year-round evergreen creative.

Script 5: Static Ad (66% Premium)

"He Sold His PT Practice for 66% Over Market Value."

Static ad targeting owners thinking about exit. Bold text, authority feel. Retargeting layer for video viewers who didn't download.

Full script body copy for all five ads available on request. You film the three video scripts on your phone in a non-clinical setting (the "freedom" visual should match the "freedom" message). We handle editing, ad build, targeting, and optimization.
