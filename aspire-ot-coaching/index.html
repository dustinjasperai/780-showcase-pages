"Think about your patients this week. How many of them are dealing with pain?"

The kid with sensory issues who also has chronic headaches. The post-surgical patient still hurting months later. The geriatric patient whose pain prevents them from engaging in daily activities. Here's the thing: OT is the profession built for this. We don't treat symptoms in isolation. We treat the whole person in the context of their daily life.
