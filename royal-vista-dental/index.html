Dr. Austin Lee

D.M.D. • Vancouver General Hospital GPR

Dr. Lee completed his Doctor of Dental Medicine in 2010 and went on to complete a General Practice Residency at Vancouver General Hospital, where he trained in oral surgery, IV sedation, and treating medically complex patients.

With 14 years of experience across Vancouver, Manitoba, Regina, and now Calgary, Dr. Lee brings a rare combination of surgical skill and gentle chairside manner to Royal Vista Dental.
