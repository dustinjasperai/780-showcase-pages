Script 3: Idea-Led - The Identity Shift (35-50 sec)

"Every practice management coach will tell you to fix your systems, optimize your schedule, and hire better. None of them start where the real problem is: how you see yourself in your own business."

If you see yourself as a clinician who also runs a business, you will stay in the exam chair all day because that is where you feel competent. The CEO-level work, strategic planning, financial architecture, team development, gets pushed to nights and weekends or does not happen at all. The shift that changes everything is moving from clinician-who-owns-a-practice to CEO-who-happens-to-be-a-clinician. That is not a semantic difference. It changes what you prioritize, what you delegate, how you spend your time, and how much of your revenue you actually keep. I call this Leadership Identity, and it is the first pillar of the CEO of You framework. It is also the step that most business coaching skips entirely, which is why their results do not stick. You can install all the systems you want, but if you are still operating as the chief-everything-officer, those systems depend on you to function.
