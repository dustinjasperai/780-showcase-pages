The Situation

Three Markets. Zero Paid Presence.

Perma-Lights operates in Kelowna, Ottawa, and Vancouver. In every one of those markets, franchise networks with marketing budgets are running ads to the same homeowners you serve.

Core Finding

Franchise Networks Are Outspending You in Every Market

Gemstone Lights has authorized dealers in all three of your markets. Trimlight has 285+ dealer locations across North America. Celebright positions as the Canadian pioneer with national coverage. These companies have marketing systems generating leads in the same neighborhoods you serve. Perma-Lights has zero paid advertising in any of them.

Reviews

5 Reviews Total

5 reviews on Trustpilot (all 5-star). No Google Business reviews. No HomeStars listing. Competitors have significantly more social proof. Every new review compounds trust. A post-install automated review request would change this within months.

Content

Video Skills Not Being Used

Cam has professional video production experience and equipment. Before/after lighting videos, nighttime showcase reels, and time-lapse installations are the highest-performing ad creative in the permanent lighting category. Most franchise dealers use static photos. This is a competitive advantage that isn't being activated.

Website

Broken Pages

The /about and /services pages return 404 errors. These are trust pages homeowners check before requesting a quote. Quick fix with immediate impact on credibility.

Seasonal

Demand Spikes Are Unmapped

Permanent lighting has natural demand peaks: pre-Christmas (Oct-Nov), spring curb appeal (Mar-Apr), and summer entertaining (Jun-Jul). Timed campaigns around these moments produce the highest conversion rates. No campaigns exist to capture any of them.
