Script 2: The Cash Flow Blind Spot Hook (35-50 sec)

"Most salon owners can tell you their revenue. Almost none of them can tell you their actual cash flow. That gap is where profit disappears."

Revenue is what comes in. Cash flow is what you actually keep after wages, rent, products, insurance, and everything else that quietly eats into your numbers. I've worked with 5,000+ salon and clinic owners across multiple countries, and the single biggest shift I see in profitability happens when an owner finally understands the difference. It's not about working more hours or adding more services. It's about knowing where your money goes and making intentional decisions about pricing, compensation, and product margins based on real numbers instead of guesses. In my book "30 Days to Beauty Business Success," I break this down into a system any owner can implement. Weekly tracking that takes 15 minutes. Pricing decisions based on your actual costs, not what the salon next door charges. Compensation structures that reward your team and protect your margins. I'm hosting a free training where I walk through the cash flow framework from the book. The specific numbers to track, where salon owners lose money without realizing it, and how to fix it within 30 days. Register below.

Script 3: The Team Retention Hook (35-50 sec)

"You know what costs more than paying your team well? Constantly replacing them. And most salon owners are doing exactly that."

Staff turnover in salons is one of the highest of any industry. An owner spends months training a stylist, building their column, getting them confident with clients, and then they leave. Sometimes for another salon. Sometimes to go independent. And the cycle starts over. The cost isn't just the lost revenue from their chair being empty. It's the disruption to your clients, the time you spend recruiting and retraining, and the toll it takes on the rest of your team's morale. I've spent decades studying what makes salon teams stay. It's not just money. It's leadership, culture, growth paths, and how valued people feel in their role. The Team and Leadership section of my "30 Days" framework gives owners a practical system for building a team that wants to grow with the business instead of looking for the next opportunity.
