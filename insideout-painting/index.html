Video Script 2
"If your house has stucco, this matters."

The Stucco Specialist Angle

"Stucco is one of the most common exterior finishes in Winnipeg, and most painting companies treat it the same as everything else. It is not.

Stucco absorbs paint differently. It cracks in our winters. If you do not prep it right and use the right application method, the paint fails within a couple of years.

At InsideOut, we use airless sprayer systems designed specifically for stucco surfaces. We have been doing this for 20 years in Winnipeg. We know what this climate does to exterior finishes and we prep for it.

We back every job with a 3-year warranty including free touch-ups. If something does not hold up, we come back and fix it."

CTA: "Send us a message if you have a stucco project."
