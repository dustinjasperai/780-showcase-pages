Our Story

Built by a family, trusted by Calgary

Amorel and Nicole Lerner started Fortress Roofing in 2001, just months after getting married. What began as a two-person operation has grown into one of Southern Alberta's most established exterior contractors, with three divisions, three office locations, and a team that takes the same care with your home as they would with their own.

Twenty-four years later, the Lerners still run every part of the business. That family accountability is why Fortress has maintained a BBB A+ rating since 2006 and built a reputation where customers call back for their second and third projects.
