Meet the Arborist

Nick Rocco built this company one honest job at a time

Nick Rocco started Tree Service Express in 2008 as a family-owned operation with one truck and a commitment to doing tree care the right way. Eighteen years later, that commitment has earned over 720 five-star reviews, TCIA accreditation, and a DOT-registered fleet of 12 vehicles with 16 crew members.

As an ISA Certified Arborist (FL-6239A) with a CTSP (Certified Tree Care Safety Professional) on staff, Nick doesn't just cut trees. He evaluates them, diagnoses them, and creates care plans that protect your property and the trees on it. That's the difference between a tree service and an arborist.
