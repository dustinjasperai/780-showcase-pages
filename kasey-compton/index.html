Script 1: The Zero to $3M Hook (45-60 sec)

"I built a group practice from zero to $3 million in less than three years. 80 providers. 5 full-time support staff. Here is the part nobody talks about."

When I started Mindsight Behavioral Group, I was a licensed counselor who knew how to help people but had no idea how to run a business. No MBA. No operations background. Just clinical training and a willingness to figure it out. The practice grew fast. We hit a million in year one and kept growing million over million after that. Today we have 80 clinical providers and only 5 full-time support staff. That ratio is not an accident. It is the result of systems. The part nobody tells you about scaling a group practice is that clinical skill does not translate to business skill. The things that made you a great therapist, listening, empathy, flexibility, are the opposite of what you need to build a scalable business. You need structure, automation, and ruthless prioritization. I wrote Fix This Next for Healthcare Providers and published two more books through Simon and Schuster because I wanted practice owners to have a framework for figuring out what to fix first. Script 2: The 5 Staff for 80 Providers Hook (35-50 sec)

"Most group practices with 80 providers have 15 to 20 support staff. We run ours with 5. The difference is not harder work. It is better systems."

When practice owners come to me, they are usually drowning in operational problems. Every decision runs through them. Their support staff is overwhelmed. They are working more hours than when they were solo practitioners. The problem is almost never the people. It is the systems. Or more accurately, the lack of them. Most group practices grow by adding bodies. When something breaks, hire someone to fix it. When that person gets overwhelmed, hire someone else. At Mindsight, we took a different approach. We built systems first, then hired only when the systems required human judgment. That is how 5 support staff manages 80 providers without chaos. I teach this in Systemizer School and through Mindsight Partners. I am hosting a free webinar that walks through the framework. How to identify the systems your practice is missing and install them in the right order. Click below to register.
