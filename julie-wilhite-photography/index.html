Script 1: The Premium Client Hook (45-60 sec)

"I built a six-figure wedding photography business in Austin. Most photographers I meet are working twice as hard for half the income. Here is why that gap exists."

Most wedding photographers compete on price. A couple inquires, asks for a quote, and the photographer gives a number they hope is low enough to book. If they lose the booking, they drop their price next time. That cycle keeps talented photographers stuck at $30,000 or $40,000 a year, working every weekend, editing every night, and wondering why the business feels so hard. I have been shooting weddings for 15 years. I built a six-figure brand serving refined clientele around the world. And the difference between my business and most photographers' businesses has nothing to do with talent. It is entirely about business strategy. Premium clients do not hire the cheapest photographer. They hire the one whose brand, experience, and positioning communicates that they are worth the investment. I am running a free training on the Six-Figure Wedding Photography System. How to attract premium clients, price without fear, and build a photography business that gives you freedom instead of burning you out. Click below to register.

Script 2: The Pricing Fear Hook (35-50 sec)

"The number one reason wedding photographers stay stuck is that they are afraid to raise their prices. I know because I had that same fear for years before I fixed it."

When I started my photography business in 2008, I priced myself at whatever I thought people would pay. I was terrified that charging more would mean empty weekends. The turning point came when I stopped competing on price and started competing on value. I elevated my brand, refined my client experience, and positioned myself as an investment, not an expense. My income doubled without shooting more weddings. I was booking fewer clients at higher prices, and every one of those clients was more appreciative, more respectful, and more likely to refer me to their friends. The photographers I coach go through the same transformation. They raise their prices, attract better clients, and build businesses that finally reflect their talent. I am hosting a free webinar on pricing strategy for wedding photographers. How to set your prices, communicate your value, and attract clients who are happy to invest in you. Register below.

Script 3: The Business Behind the Camera Hook (35-50 sec)

"I have met hundreds of incredibly talented wedding photographers who can barely pay their bills. The problem is never the photography. It is the business behind the camera."

Being a great photographer and running a great photography business are two different skills. And most photography education focuses entirely on the first one. Nobody teaches you how to price your work, how to market to premium clients, how to build vendor relationships that fill your calendar, or how to create systems that free you from editing until midnight. I spent 15 years building a six-figure wedding photography brand. I learned the business side through trial and error, making every mistake along the way. And now I help other photographers skip the painful parts and go straight to the system that works. Courses, one-on-one coaching, and a community of photographers who are on the same journey. Everything built on what I actually did to build my business, not theory from someone who has never booked a wedding. I am running a free training on building a profitable photography business.
