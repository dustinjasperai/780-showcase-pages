3 Scripts Written for LK Installations
Each script is based on what's converting in the permanent lighting market right now. You film on your phone, we handle everything else.
Video - 45-60s

Script 1: Authority and Credentials

Talking head filmed on phone. Kent speaks directly to camera.
Hook
"Most people don't realize they're climbing a ladder every year for a problem that's already been solved."
Body

"I'm Kent with LK Installations here in Campbell River. We're a certified Gemstone Lights dealer, and we've completed over 150 installations across Vancouver Island.

What we do is install a permanent lighting system along your roofline that you control from your phone. Pick any color, any pattern, for any occasion. Christmas, Halloween, Canada Day, or just a warm white glow every evening.

The system is rated IP67 waterproof, built with LEDs that last over 50,000 hours, and it's engineered right here in Canada for our weather. No more dragging out boxes of tangled lights every November. No more climbing ladders in the rain.

One installation. Every holiday covered. And it looks clean during the day because the track blends right into your soffit."

Call to Action

"If you're in Campbell River or anywhere on the Island, send us a message or visit lkinstallations.ca for a free quote."

Script 2: One Install, Every Holiday

Phone-filmed montage with voiceover. Show the same house in different color modes.
Hook
"One install. Every holiday. Watch this."
Body

"Here's what permanent lighting actually looks like throughout the year.

Christmas? Red, green, and white. Done. Halloween? Orange and purple. One tap on your phone. Canada Day? Red and white across your entire roofline. Regular Tuesday night? Warm white so your house looks incredible from the street.

This is one set of lights that never comes down, never tangles, and never needs a ladder. You just open the app and pick what you want."

Call to Action

"LK Installations, Campbell River. Free quotes at lkinstallations.ca."
