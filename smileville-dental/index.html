What We Do

Full-service care, all under one roof.

With four dentists on staff, Smileville handles almost every dental need in-house. Cleanings, implants, root canals, Invisalign, sedation, and sports dentistry, without sending you across the city for referrals.

General & Family Dentistry

Cleanings, exams, fillings, and preventive care for every member of the family. From a child's first visit to adult restorative work.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Porcelain veneers, teeth whitening, and smile makeovers designed to enhance what you already have without looking overdone.

Dental Implants & Surgery

Full implant placement, wisdom tooth extraction, and bone grafting handled in-house by Dr. Dobbala's surgical training from NYU.

Invisalign & Clear Aligners

Straighten your smile with Invisalign and Suresmile aligners. Treatment plans tailored to your schedule, budget, and goals.

Sedation Dentistry

Oral and IV sedation for patients with dental anxiety or complex procedures. Comfortable care without the stress.

Emergency & Sports Dentistry

Same-day emergency appointments, custom athletic mouthguards, and sideline care. Official dental partner of the Calgary Hitmen.

Why Smileville

What sets us apart in Calgary dentistry.

Beyond the clinical work, the experience of coming to Smileville is what keeps families coming back year after year and bringing the rest of the family with them.

01

Open When You Are

Weekdays until 7pm and Saturdays until 4pm at both locations. No more taking time off work or pulling kids out of school for a routine checkup.

02

Free Parking at Both Locations

Two hours of free surface parking at our Beltline location and onsite parking at Elbow Drive. Rare for downtown Calgary, and it matters when you're rushing between meetings.

03

Four Dentists, Zero Referrals

Most clinics send you elsewhere for implants, root canals, or surgery. Our four-dentist roster handles almost everything in-house, so you stay with the team that knows your history.

04

Sedation for Anxious Patients

Oral and IV sedation are available for patients who dread the chair, complex procedures, or long treatment visits. Comfort isn't a luxury here. It's the baseline.

05

In-House Direct Financing

Larger treatments like implants or full cosmetic work can be financed directly through the clinic. No third-party applications, no surprise interest rates.

Trusted by Calgary Athletes
