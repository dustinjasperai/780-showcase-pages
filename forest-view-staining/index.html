Script 1

The Proprietary Product Ad

"Most staining companies use whatever product is cheapest at the store. I spent 10 years making my own."

My name is Dan Schwade. I own Forest View Staining. I have been staining decks and fences in Toronto for 26 years.

About 10 years ago I got frustrated with the cleaning products on the market. They were harsh. They damaged wood. They were not safe for pets, gardens, or families. So I started developing my own.

It took a decade of testing and refining. The result is "A Touch of Dan." My own line of wood cleaning products. Ontario-made. COSMOS-standard organic ingredients. Biodegradable. Sold on Amazon Canada and in retail stores.

I do not know of another staining company in Toronto that makes their own products. The reason I did it is simple. I wanted something that actually works without destroying the wood or the environment.

When you hire Forest View, you are not just getting a staining crew. You are getting 26 years of hands-on expertise and products I developed specifically for the job.
