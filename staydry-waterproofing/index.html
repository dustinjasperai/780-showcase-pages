Seasonal Urgency

Spring Thaw Drives Peak Demand Every Year

Every spring, Michigan's snowmelt creates massive groundwater surges. Freeze-thaw cycles crack foundations all winter, and spring is when the water finds those cracks. Hydrostatic pressure builds as frozen ground thaws from the surface down, pushing water against foundation walls. Nobody is running paid ads targeting this annual urgency. A campaign timed to the spring thaw would reach homeowners at their highest point of intent.
