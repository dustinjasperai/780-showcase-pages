5 Ad Scripts Built Around the "3x Exit" Story

Here are the ready-to-film ad scripts I mentioned in my email. They put your strongest proof point in front of every PT practice owner scrolling Facebook who's wondering what their business is actually worth.

Quick walkthrough
The scripts

3 Video Scripts + 2 Static Ads

All five drive strategy call bookings. You film the video scripts on your phone. No production crew needed.

Script 1: The 3x Exit Hook

"I sold my physical therapy clinics for 3x the national average. Here's what I built that made a buyer pay that premium."

On-camera, 55-70 seconds. Your strongest cold traffic hook. Tells the exit story, introduces the three things that create enterprise value, and drives to strategy call. This is the lead creative.

Script 2: The "Get Out of Treatment" Hook

"If you own a PT clinic and you're still treating 30 hours a week, you don't have a business. You have a job you can't quit."

On-camera, 45-55 seconds. Targets the emotional pain point of being stuck in treatment. Shows the path from solo operator to real business owner. Appeals to overwhelmed owners.

Script 3: The Scaling Hook

"Going from one clinic to two was the hardest thing I did as a practice owner. Going from two to three was easy. Here's why."

On-camera, 50-60 seconds. Targets ambitious owners thinking about expansion. Systems-focused. Narrower audience but highest intent buyers.

Script 4: Static Ad (Exit Value)

"He Sold His PT Clinics for 3x the National Average."

Static feed ad. Authority positioning. Clean professional image with bold text overlay. Drives strategy call bookings from scroll.

Script 5: Static Ad (Stuck in Treatment)

"Still Treating 30 Hours a Week in Your Own Clinic?"

Static ad targeting overwhelmed owner-operators. Relatable pain point as the visual focus. Retargeting layer for video viewers who didn't book.
