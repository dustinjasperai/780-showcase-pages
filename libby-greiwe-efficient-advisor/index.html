"If you're a financial advisor working 50 or 60 hours a week and your revenue has been flat for 2 years, the problem isn't your clients. It's your systems."

When your client onboarding takes 3 hours instead of 10, when your meeting prep is a 15-minute checklist instead of a 2-hour scramble, when your follow-up is automated instead of manual, everything changes. "The first 100 days with a new client either sets up a lifetime relationship or a 3-year churn. Most advisors wing it."

Client onboarding is the most underleveraged system in most advisory practices. You spend months earning the prospect's trust, they finally sign, and then the experience goes from high-touch to inconsistent. I built a First 100 Days system that turned my onboarding into a competitive advantage. New clients felt taken care of from day one. They referred faster. They stayed longer.
