Who Is Running Webinars and Live Events in Your Space

Clinicians thinking about building a business don't trust generic marketing advice. They've spent years in evidence-based environments. That's exactly why webinars are gaining traction in this niche. Here's who's doing it and how.

EntreMD

Dr. Una | Paediatrician turned business coach | US-based | Inc. 5000 (#315)
Their Live Event

The Visibility Formula Workshop. A free, multi-day online workshop teaching physician entrepreneurs how to build business visibility. Each day covers a different pillar: becoming known, amplifying your message, positioning for ideal clients, and building sustainable marketing systems.

Their Funnel

Free workshop captures contact info. Multi-day format builds commitment. Participants are directed to the EntreMD Business School (three tiers: Advance, Grow, Scale). Secondary lead magnet: free masterclass. Entry-point book on Amazon. 700K+ podcast downloads, 520+ episodes, active Facebook advertising.

Biggest player in the space. The ecosystem approach works. But everything is built for American physicians: private practice revenue, CEO mindset, 7-figure language. NHS clinicians looking at this would feel like it wasn't built for them. Because it wasn't.

Physician Coaching Institute

Dr. Francine Gaillour | MD/MBA | US-based | ICF Level 1 Accredited
Their Live Event

A free recorded Information Webinar (42 minutes) walking prospective students through the PCI curriculum, the coaching profession, and how physician coaching fits into healthcare. Covers training pathways, certification requirements, cohort structure, and tuition.

Their Funnel

Free PDF lead magnet ("Choosing Your Coaching Niche in Healthcare: 20+ Expertise Areas") feeds into webinar. After watching, CTA is a 1-on-1 Enrollment Discussion requiring a preliminary application. Three programme tiers with CME credits. Active Facebook advertising.

Professional and information-dense. The qualification step positions the programme as selective. But PCI trains physicians to become coaches, not founders. A clinician who wants to launch a venture won't find that guidance here. Entirely US-focused.

BootstrapMD / Momentum MD

Dr. Mike Woo-Ming & Dr. Peter Kim | 20+ years physician entrepreneurship | US-based
Their Live Event

The Momentum MD Mastermind includes monthly group coaching calls, quarterly sessions with both founders, monthly all-cohort strategy sessions, and two private in-person retreats per year in Southern California. Application-based, limited to 12 members per cohort.

Their Funnel

Content (330+ podcast episodes, Passive Income MD blog) builds awareness. Landing page positions around eight life dimensions with detailed testimonials. Apply-to-join exclusivity creates natural scarcity.

Intimate model with genuine mentorship. The 12-person cohort and in-person retreats build real bonds. But this is for physicians who already have established businesses. A clinician at the idea stage is several steps behind where this programme starts. US-centric. Premium investment level.

NHS Clinical Entrepreneur Programme

NHS England | Government-backed | Free | UK-based
Their Live Event

CPD-accredited "Pit Stops" (immersive 2-day events, blended online and in-person), regular workshops, webinars, and 1-on-1 clinics with experts. Events scheduled on evenings and weekends to fit clinical schedules. One-year structured programme with mentoring.

Their Funnel

Application-based, free. Annual cohort with limited spots. Accepted participants get mentoring from clinical, academic, commercial, and public sector leaders. Recordings provided for missed sessions.

Free, NHS-sanctioned, and gives clinicians institutional credibility. But it's built for healthcare innovation (medtech, digital health, service redesign), not for building personal brands, coaching practices, or expert-led businesses. A springboard, not a runway.

SurgeonMasters

Physician coaching & wellness | ICF Level 1 Accredited | US-based
Their Live Event

Monthly live practice coaching sessions on Zoom: first Thursday (6-8 PM Eastern) and third Sunday (8-10 PM Eastern). 5-6 hours of structured practice coaching monthly. Flexible scheduling with monthly polls. On-demand content between live sessions.

Their Funnel

Website explains the programme. On-demand content provides foundation, live sessions provide practice. ICF credential pathway for graduates. Positioned around flexible scheduling for physician schedules.

Smart scheduling model that acknowledges physician reality. But like PCI, this trains physicians to be coaches, not founders. US-centric scheduling and "SurgeonMasters" branding narrows the perceived audience. Nurses and allied health professionals would self-select out.

The Gap

What's Missing From This Market

No UK/NHS-Focused Programme

Every major player is US-based. They talk about private practice revenue and American insurance systems. NHS clinicians operate in a completely different world: portfolio careers, public healthcare, different funding pathways. Nobody is teaching through that lens.

Two Camps Missing the Middle

On one side: programmes that train physicians to become coaches. On the other: programmes for physicians who already have businesses. The clinician who's still practising with an idea and wants a structured path from bedside to business? Underserved.

No Real Stories + Frameworks

Nobody is combining real clinician founder stories with practical, teachable frameworks in a live format. 45 episodes of The Clinician Founder Podcast have proven this content resonates. A webinar that packages it would be unlike anything else in the market.

The A-G-A Framework

Awareness → Groundwork → Action

Most clinician founders skip straight to Action. They buy a domain, set up social media, maybe record a few videos. Then they stall because they never did the first two steps.

  • Awareness: Understand your strengths, market, and unfair advantage
  • Groundwork: Validate your idea, build your audience, design your offer
  • Action: Launch, test, iterate, grow without working another 60-hour week
ValueCore IP. Clinicians are trained to act. That instinct works against them here.
Slide 12 of 25
STEP 1

Awareness

As a clinician, you already have: credibility, a network, pattern recognition, decision-making under pressure, and a real understanding of healthcare problems outsiders can only guess at.

Your job isn't to become an entrepreneur who happens to have a medical degree. It's to become a clinician founder who uses clinical strengths as business advantages.

ValueReframes imposter syndrome. Their clinical training IS their advantage.
Slide 13 of 25
STEP 2

Groundwork

1. Who specifically am I helping? Not "doctors." What speciality? What career stage? What frustration?

2. What do they actually want? Talk to 10 people before you build anything.

3. What's the simplest version I can test? A workshop. A PDF guide. Something you can deliver in 30 days.

ValueMedical analogy: don't prescribe without diagnosis. Validate first.

The 5 Mistakes That Stall Most Clinician Founders

ValueSection header

The 5 Mistakes

  • 1. Waiting until you "know enough." You'll never feel ready. Start with clinical knowledge and learn business by doing.
  • 2. Trying to build alone. Medicine trained independence. Business rewards collaboration.
  • 3. Copying what works in America. What works in Texas won't work for an NHS registrar in Norwich.
  • 4. Treating your venture like a hobby. Hobby time = hobby results. 5 structured hours a week changes everything.
  • 5. Not charging for your expertise. Free advice is valued at exactly what it costs.
