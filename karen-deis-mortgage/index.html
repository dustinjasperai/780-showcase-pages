Script 2: The Mortgage Scripts Hook (35-50 sec)

"Most loan officers lose deals because they do not know what to say at the moment it matters most. Rate objections, Realtor prospecting, past client follow-ups. They improvise, and it costs them."

A borrower calls with a rate objection. You stumble through a response. A Realtor asks why they should work with you. You default to talking about your rates and your service. A past client has not heard from you in a year. You avoid calling because it feels awkward. These are the moments that determine your production. And most loan officers handle them by winging it. I wrote The Little Book of Mortgage Scripts to solve this problem. It contains tested, proven scripts for every common situation you face as a loan officer. Rate objections, Realtor prospecting, referral requests, past client reactivation, and dozens more. When you have a script, you stop thinking about what to say and start listening to what the borrower is telling you. That shift changes everything.
