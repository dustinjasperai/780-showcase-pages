The COR + BBB Trust Ad

"Before you hire a paving company, ask two questions. Are they COR certified? And what does their BBB record look like?"

There are a lot of paving companies in Calgary. Some of them show up in the spring, quote low, and disappear before the first frost. Others have been around a while but cut corners on base preparation because it saves them time.

Holloway Paving is COR certified. That is Alberta's Certificate of Recognition for workplace safety. It means our crews follow documented safety procedures on every job. It is not optional for us. It is how we operate.

We are also BBB A+ accredited since 2020. That accreditation is not free. You pay for it. And you only keep it if you maintain a clean record.

Four generations of paving experience. Residential driveways, commercial parking lots, municipal projects. We do the full job from excavation through to seal coating and line painting. And if you need snow removal in the winter, we handle that too.
