About Pain Updated

Care from someone who treats, teaches, and researches recovery.

Pain Updated was founded by Lisa Jasper, PT, PhD — a physiotherapist with nearly three decades of clinical experience and a doctorate in rehabilitation science from the University of Alberta, where she also serves as an Adjunct Professor in the Faculty of Rehabilitation Medicine.

Lisa is one of very few clinicians who do all three: treat patients, teach the next generation of physiotherapists, and publish the research — including peer-reviewed studies on recovery after total knee replacement. Her doctoral work focused on physical activity and function in older adults following joint replacement.

Pain Updated brings that expertise to you virtually, so you can prepare for surgery and rehab on your own schedule, from the comfort of home — with guidance grounded in evidence, not guesswork.
