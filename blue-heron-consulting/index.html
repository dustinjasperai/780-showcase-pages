Script 1: The 339 Practices Hook (45-60 sec)

"We have coached 339 veterinary practices. The ones that struggle all have the same problem. It is not clinical skill. It is that the owner is doing everything."

Most veterinary practice owners went to school to practice medicine. Nobody taught them how to run a business. Practice owners spend less than 20 percent of their time working on the business. The rest is spent working in it, putting out fires. Blue Heron Consulting has coached 339 practices over the last decade. We have guided 94 veterinarians to practice ownership. Our clients typically see 2 to 3x increases in net income because we do not just coach on one thing. Script 2: The $1.76M to $3.49M Hook (35-50 sec)

"One of our clients went from $1.76 million in revenue to $3.49 million. Same practice. Same location. Same number of doctors. Different systems."

When this practice came to us, the owner was burned out. Revenue had plateaued. The team was disorganized. There was no financial visibility and no leadership structure. We paired them with two coaches, one for medical operations and one for business operations. We restructured their pricing, installed financial tracking through our bookkeeping team, and rebuilt their hiring process. Within two years, revenue nearly doubled. Net income increased 2.8x. The owner went from working 60 hours a week to 42. This is what 360-degree coaching does. It is not advice on one topic. It is a full team supporting every dimension of your practice simultaneously.
