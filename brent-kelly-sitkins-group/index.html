Script 2: The "Buy Back Your Time" Hook (35-50 sec)

"The average agency owner spends 60% of their time on activities that don't grow the agency. Renewals, service issues, HR problems. You're a highly paid administrator, not a business leader."

The best producers in our programs increase their book of business by 25-50% in the first year. Not because they work more hours, but because they stop doing things that don't produce revenue. At Sitkins, we call it "buying back your time." Every hour you spend on a renewal that could be handled by your service team is an hour you didn't spend on a new business opportunity worth 10x more. The math is simple. If your close rate is 30% and your average account is $10K in commission, every qualified appointment you take is worth $3K.
