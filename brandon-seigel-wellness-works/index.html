Script 3: Idea-Led - "Your Billing Company Doesn't Work for You"

"Here's something most therapy practice owners don't think about. Your billing company gets paid whether your collections go up or down."

They charge you a percentage of what they process, not a percentage of what they actually recover. So denied claims, delayed appeals, patient balances sitting for 90 days? Those aren't their problem. And most of them process claims once a week. Think about what that means. A claim that could have been submitted Monday doesn't go out until Friday. Multiply that across hundreds of claims a month and you're looking at weeks of delayed cash flow, every single month. I spent 15 years watching third-party billing agencies do this to therapy practices. They chase the lowest hanging fruit, skip the hard stuff, and call it a service. So I built a different model. We process claims daily. We have certified coders reviewing every submission. And we charge 6% of what we actually collect for you. If your collections don't go up, our fee doesn't either. We call it a Deconstructed MSO. Everything a management service organization provides, without taking any ownership of your practice. Billing, HR, coaching, operations. You keep your practice.
