Script 2: The Insurance Trap Hook (35-50 sec)

"I used to work six days a week seeing 30 clients. I was earning $140,000 a year and completely burning out. A friend told me I was going to destroy myself. That conversation changed everything."

When I was a staff therapist at a group practice, I was earning good money on paper. But I was exhausted. Six-day weeks. No margin. No autonomy over my schedule or my fees. A psychiatrist friend sat me down and told me the pace was unsustainable. She was right. I left and built a solo cash-pay practice. Within a year I had replaced my income working fewer hours with complete control over my schedule. The difference was not working harder. It was building a practice where I set my own fees, chose my own clients, and did not depend on insurance companies to determine what my time was worth. I have since helped 3,000 therapists make this same transition. I am hosting a free webinar on the cash-pay practice model. How to set your fees, fill your caseload without insurance panels, and build a practice that does not burn you out. Click below to register.
