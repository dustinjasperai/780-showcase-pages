Script 2: The 'Limiting Beliefs' Hook (35-50 sec)

"The biggest obstacle to growing your advisory practice isn't your marketing. It isn't your niche. It isn't your tech stack. It's what you believe is possible."

I've coached hundreds of top advisors. The ones who transform their practices fastest aren't the ones who implement the most tactics. They're the ones who change what they believe about their business and themselves. Most advisors carry beliefs they picked up in their first year. You have to work long hours. You have to say yes to every client. You can't charge what you're worth. You'll lose clients if you raise fees. None of it is true. But it feels true because it's been reinforced for years. The Limitless framework starts by identifying and dismantling these beliefs, then rebuilding your practice around what's actually possible. I'm hosting a free webinar on the mindset shifts that separate advisors doing $300K and working 60 hours from advisors doing $1M and taking 100 days off. Register below.
