Financial Systems: Know Your Numbers First

  • Monthly view of margins by job type, overhead coverage, and cash position
  • Not "my bookkeeper does my taxes once a year" -- that's archaeology, not financial management
  • What to track, how often, and what each number actually tells you
  • You can't fix what you can't measure, and you can't measure what you don't track
