Financial Systems: Know Your Numbers First

  • Monthly view of margins by job type, overhead coverage, and cash position
  • Not "my bookkeeper does my taxes once a year" -- that's archaeology, not financial management
  • What to track, how often, and what each number actually tells you
  • You can't fix what you can't measure, and you can't measure what you don't track
Contractor Coaching
10

What Good Financial Systems Actually Look Like

What Most Contractors Have

  • Bookkeeper does taxes once a year
  • No idea which job types are most profitable
  • Cash in the bank = "we're doing fine"
  • Surprised by overhead every quarter

What You Need

  • Monthly margins by job type
  • Overhead as a percentage, tracked monthly
  • Cash position and projected vs. actual
  • Knowing you're profitable, not hoping
