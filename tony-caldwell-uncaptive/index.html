Script 2: The "Captive Ceiling" Hook (35-50 sec)

"If you're a captive agent, you already know the ceiling is real. You've hit it, or you can see it coming. The question is whether you have a plan to get out before it costs you another five years."

The problem isn't ambition. Most captive agents I talk to are excellent at what they do. The problem is the structure. You're building a book of business on someone else's platform, with limited markets, and a brand that doesn't travel with you if you leave. Going independent changes everything about the math. Your own book. Multiple markets. A business that has actual resale value. The ability to hire, scale, and build something that's genuinely yours. The agents I've worked with who made the jump, with a plan and the right support structure behind them, almost universally wish they'd done it sooner.
