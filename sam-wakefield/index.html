The rest of the funnel

What Wraps the Masterclass

A webinar on its own is just another video, and your podcast already gives reps plenty to listen to. What turns it into a funnel is the path around it: a page that gets the right rep to register, and a follow-up sequence that brings him back to watch and then points him at the free podcast and the discovery call you already run. All of it in your brand and voice, value-first and fully compliant, framed around trust and the psychology-first method, never income or close-rate claims.

A clean landing page in your blue-and-dark brand that frames the masterclass for the rep tired of pressure and pushback, and captures the registration. The front door for cold and retargeted traffic.
Step two
The Email Follow-Up
A short sequence in your voice that confirms the registration, brings no-shows back to watch, and after the masterclass points to the free podcast and a free discovery call.

One path, end to end: register, watch, follow up, book the call. Built once, runs evergreen, fed by paid traffic in front of and behind the audience you already own.
